Simple delicious beef tacos

Ingredients

  • 1.5Kg beef in 5cm pieces
  • 5 salam leaves
  • 2 sticks of lemongrass
  • 25gr dried coconut flakes
  • 200ml pineapple juice or oj
  • 1liter santen (coconut milk)
  • 4 red peppers or 2 habanero
  • 3tbps corianderseeds
  • 5cm piece of laos grated
  • 10cm ginger grated
  • 1tbsp koenjit
  • 10 cloves of garlic
  • 4 red peppers or 2 habanero
  • Lomboks to taste (optional)

Instructions

  1. In a kitchen mixer add peppers,garlic,laos,ginger,koenjit and the koenjit and blend to a paste
  2. Take the paste and rub it into the beef
  3. Take the beef and santen and bring to a softboilboil
  4. Add the salamleaves and lemongrass(bruised) and simmer on low for 2 hours
  5. Taste add optional lomboks and salt and simmer 1 more hour stirring occasionally
  6. When the liquid has evaporated and oil floats to the top and the meat is goldenbrown its done
  7. Toast the coconut flakes and stir it into the stew
  8. During the cooling down the meat will soak up the liquid we rehydrate this with the pineapple juice
